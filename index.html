The Story

London. 1908.

The world's no.1 stamp collector, Francis Fox, is framed for a murder that he didn't commit.

To clear his name, he investigates the murder and uncovers a conspiracy stretching from London's darkest alleyways to the highest levels of society.

Every clue leads to another secret… and someone will kill to keep them hidden.
